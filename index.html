Work with Melisa

Personalised nutrition support for athletes, active women, and everyday life.

My name is Melisa. I am a Sports Nutrition Specialist based in London, offering online consultations for athletes and individuals who want clearer, healthier routines.

Through my experience working in hospitals and with people from different backgrounds, I have supported clients in developing balanced eating habits that feel realistic and sustainable. There are no fad diets or extreme methods, only evidence-based guidance that fits your lifestyle.

Nutrition affects energy, wellbeing, performance, confidence, and long-term health. With the right guidance, your everyday choices can become simpler, calmer, and more consistent.

What I stand for

Nutrition is not just a plan. It is clarity, balance, and mindset.

My mission is to build a strong and positive mindset, enabling individuals to follow nutrition programmes aligned with their goals. I help you understand what your body truly needs so you can move forward with confidence and consistency.

Recipe ideas

Simple meals for energy, recovery, and consistency.

Breakfast

Protein berry yoghurt bowl

Greek yoghurt, berries, banana, oats, chia seeds, and a drizzle of honey.

  • High protein
  • Easy pre-training option
  • Ready in 5 minutes
Lunch

Chicken quinoa power plate

Grilled chicken, quinoa, avocado, cucumber, tomatoes, greens, and lemon olive oil dressing.

  • Balanced carbs, protein, and fats
  • Great for busy work days
  • Supports recovery
Snack

Evening craving plate

Apple slices, peanut butter, cottage cheese or yoghurt, cinnamon, and a few dark chocolate pieces.

  • Satisfying and structured
  • Helps reduce grazing
  • Sweet without feeling restrictive
